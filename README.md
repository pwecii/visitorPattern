<h1>Telco Promo System – Visitor Design Pattern</h1>

<h2>Project Overview</h2>
<p>
Imagine you are searching for a new mobile plan for your smartphone. 
Three major telecommunication providers are offering different promotional deals:
<strong>Smart</strong>, <strong>Globe</strong>, and <strong>Ditto</strong>.
</p>

<p>
This project implements the <strong>Visitor Design Pattern</strong> in Java to model
different telco subscription plans and their promotional offers.
The system separates the object structure (telco plans) from the operations
performed on them (data allowance display and unlimited call/text offers).
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Available Telco Plans</h2>

<h3>🔵 Smart</h3>
<p><strong>Data Allowance:</strong> 15 GB</p>
<p><strong>Price:</strong> ₱500 per month</p>
<p><strong>Unlimited Calls & Texts:</strong> Not Included</p>
<p>
Calls and texts are charged per use.
</p>

<h3>🟢 Globe</h3>
<p><strong>Data Allowance:</strong> 10 GB</p>
<p><strong>Price:</strong> ₱450 per month</p>
<p><strong>Unlimited Calls & Texts:</strong> Included (within Globe network only)</p>
<p>
Calls and texts to other networks are charged extra.
</p>

<h3>🟣 Ditto</h3>
<p><strong>Data Allowance:</strong> 8 GB</p>
<p><strong>Price:</strong> ₱400 per month</p>
<p><strong>Unlimited Calls & Texts:</strong> Included (all networks nationwide)</p>
